    பிடியதன் உருவுமை கொளமிகு கரியது..

    19 May, 2019 ThirukadaiKaapu


    பிடியதன் உருவுமை கொளமிகு கரியது
    வடிகொடு தனதடி வழிபடும் அவரிடர்
    கடி கணபதிவர அருளினன் மிகுகொடை
    வடிவினர் பயில்வலி வலமுறை இறையே!


    Pidiyadhan uruvumai kolamigu kariyadhu
    Vadikodu thanadhadi val.ipadum avaridar
    KadigaNa padhivara varulinan migukodai
    Vadivinar payilvali valamurai yiraiyey!

    Back Ground

    Gajamugasuram was a great Shiva bhakt, he did deep penance and received boons from the Lord. Gajamugasuram was denied for a boon of immortality, however he wanted to make sure that death doesn't happen for him easily, he got a boon in a way such that he will not be able to be killed by a man, animal or any celestial body and with any weapons. God said Thathaasthu. After receiving the boons his native evil mind started displaying its asura nature and became unmanageable menace to everyone.
    Uma maheshwari the source of clemency took a form of female elephant, Lord Maheshwaran in valivalam had a form of male elephant and both merged to manifest into Lord Ganapathy (head of bhoodhas) to kill the asura. Lord Ganapathy a manifestation of compassinoate mother Uma , didnt kill the asura instead cleared the ego and vanity of Gajamugasuram when he tried to elude from the almighty in the form of bandicoot; when he yielded to the the lord, he was thoroughly blessed and was made as Lord's vahana.
    Not just the asura but for all devotees who surrenders to his lotus feet will be blessed, Hail Ganapathy !
