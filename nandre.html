    21 May, 2019 Abirami Anthathi


    நன்றே வருகினும், தீதே விளைகினும், நான் அறிவது
    ஒன்றேயும் இல்லை; உனக்கே பரம் எனக்கு உள்ள எல்லாம்
    அன்றே உனதென்று அளித்து விட்டேன்; அழியாத குணக்
    குன்றே! அருட்கடலே! இமவான் பெற்ற கோமளமே!!


    Nandre varuginum Theedhae vilaiginum naan arivadhu
    ondreyum illai unakkae param ennaku ulla ellam
    andre unadhendru alithu vittaen aziyaadha guna
    kundre arutkadalae imavan pettra komalamae

    Back Ground

    Peace is a state where the mind contracts the ability of being congruent between the joy and sarrow, the good and the bad and about any sort of extremes.
    Abirami pattar has given a poem seeking for yet another needed blessings one should earn. He seeks to the Almighty: Whether it is good or bad that comes in his way and because everything of his life was handed over to the mother Abirami long ago and became innocent of what is happening, he cries to the mother who has a blessing heart, the ocean of clemency, the komalam who is ever ephebic, to become solely responsible for whatever happens to him.
    Such a beautiful poem, what else one may need when our Mother Abirami herself start taking care of us, so that we always would be in the state of ecstacy like a child. !
